Youniverse Guide

Storyline

After emerging from somewhere unknown, you are greeted by The Void. It will require some clever actions in order to achieve anything in this empty canvas. Luckily, The Void seems eager to get started.

Objective

Your objective in Youniverse is to break through the limits restraining your accumulation of power, but be warned, these limits exist for a reason

Energy

Your progress is measured by Energy Collection. To start, you will have to manually click to generate Energy, but eventually you will have tools to generate Energy for you.

Abilities

Power grows exponentially, and so shall you with the abilities you gain to help boost your Energy Production

Limits

Limits act as benchmarks and achievements for the game, necessary to progress along the storyline and unlock abilities.
